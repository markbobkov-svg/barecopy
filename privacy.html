Privacy

Short version: your files never leave your device, and we don't collect personal data.

Files

Barecopy runs entirely in your browser. Files you drop on the page are read and processed locally by JavaScript. They are never transmitted, uploaded, or stored by us. You can verify this: load the page, disconnect from the internet, and the tool keeps working.

License keys (Pro)

If you activate a Pro subscription, your license key is sent to our payment provider's license API (Polar Software Inc.) when you activate and then periodically (about once a day) to confirm the subscription is still active. The key is stored in your browser's local storage. No file data ever accompanies these requests.

Payments

Purchases are processed by Polar as merchant of record. Your payment details go to Polar and their payment processors, never to us. See Polar's privacy policy for details.

Cookies & analytics

Barecopy sets no cookies and runs no advertising or cross-site tracking scripts. To count visits we use Vercel Web Analytics, which is cookieless and privacy-friendly: it records only anonymous, aggregate page views (which page, referrer, approximate country, device type) and does not identify you, fingerprint your device, or follow you across sites. It never sees your files, file names, or their metadata.
